Quick answers before you join

How do I join Bewafa?

Click any "Join Discord" button on this page or head to discord.gg/bewafa. You'll need a free Discord account.

Is it free to join?

Yes — Bewafa is completely free to join and always will be.

What kind of giveaways do you run?

We run frequent giveaways including Discord Nitro, Litecoin (LTC) and other prizes during events.

Is the server active?

Very. With 10k+ members, chats and voice channels stay active around the clock.

How can I become staff?

Stay active, be friendly and helpful, and keep an eye out for staff applications announced in the server.
